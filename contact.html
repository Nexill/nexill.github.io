Setup & Hardware Freedom

Which operating systems and devices does Nexill POS run on?

Nexill is a pure Windows application. Because it runs natively on your machine—not in a browser—it reacts instantly. Mac support is actively in development.

Am I forced to buy proprietary barcode scanners or receipt printers?

No. We don't force you to buy expensive proprietary scanners. Nexill runs with any standard USB barcode scanner or receipt printer you can plug into a Windows PC. Total hardware freedom.

Inventory & Products

Will the system tell me when I need to reorder stock?

Yes. Nexill features an active, multi-layer notification system. It immediately triggers low-stock alerts to you and your staff the moment an item drops below your customized threshold, ensuring you never miss a sale due to an empty shelf.

Data, Analytics & Staffing

Can I lock down what my cashiers are allowed to see?

Yes. Nexill comes standard with comprehensive, granular employee permission settings. Owners can grant cashiers strictly the capabilities required to ring up transactions, completely locking down sensitive sales analytics and pricing controls.

How deep do the sales reports go?

Deeply. We don't just show you what sold; we show you what made profit. Compare categories instantly (e.g. Snacks vs. Beverages) and track exactly which items are generating revenue versus which are just taking up shelf space.
